# Contributing to the development of Battlestar Arena.

First and foremost, thanks for showing enough interest to contribute to Battlestar Arena. Please remember that these are merely
guidelines and not rules. Do your best to follow them, but don't expect any sort of repercussions. That being said, please be
respectful of the community.

As the development of this project moves forward, and more opportunities for contribution become available to the public. This
document will be improved on. It will describe some prelimiary information, show how you can contribute to the project, and
discuss important style guidelines for contributing.
